ABOUT

Vintage goods are items that are more than 20 years old and are still in good condition or usable. Typically, vintage items have historical, aesthetic, and unique values that make them highly sought after by collectors or fans of antique and retro items. To be considered vintage, an item must have an interesting history or story to be found in the past.

Because of their high value and their own charm, vintage items are often used as collectibles, investments, or even as personal items used by people who like a retro or classic lifestyle. The Vintage 'Orchidea introduces classic products that have been around for a long time but have beautiful value because they are more than 10 years old but unique and beautiful.
